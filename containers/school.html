KTL (Kazakh Turkish Liceum)

What is KTL?

KTL stands for Kazakh-Turkish Lyceum, a network of high-level secondary schools in Kazakhstan founded in cooperation with Turkey. These schools are well known for their strong academic programs, especially in math, science, and English.

KTL in Oskemen (Ust-Kamenogorsk)

  • Offers strong education in mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and English.
  • Classes are usually taught in three languages: Kazakh, English, and Russian.
  • Known for strict discipline and academic excellence.
  • Some subjects are taught by Turkish teachers, especially science and English.
  • Students often participate in international competitions and olympiads (IMO, IPhO, etc.).
  • The school has a boarding option, so some students live on campus.
