KBTU "Kazakh-British Technical University"

What is KBTU?

KBTU stands for Kazakh-British Technical University, located in Almaty, Kazakhstan. It's one of the top technical universities in the country, with a strong focus on engineering, IT, economics, and business. KBTU is known for its international standards, English-taught programs, and partnerships with UK universities and global companies.
